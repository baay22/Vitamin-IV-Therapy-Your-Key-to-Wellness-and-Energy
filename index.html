What is Vitamin IV Therapy?

This therapy involves the intravenous administration of a carefully formulated blend of essential vitamins, minerals, and nutrients. NAD+ Infusion: Advanced IV Nutritional Therapy

One of the most advanced forms of IV nutritional therapy is NAD+ infusion. Nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide (NAD+) is a coenzyme essential to energy metabolism and is found in all living cells. NAD+ directly and indirectly influences many vital cellular functions, including:

  • Metabolic Pathways: Metabolic pathways are the processes that our cells use to break down food and turn it into energy. These pathways also help create the building blocks the body needs to grow and repair.
  • DNA Repair: DNA repair is the process where our cells fix damage to their DNA. This helps prevent problems like mutations, which can lead to diseases.
  • Chromosome Remodeling: Chromosome remodeling is the process where the structure of chromosomes is changed. This helps control how genes are turned on or off in our cells.
  • Cellular Aging: Cellular aging happens when cells lose their ability to function properly over time. This can lead to the breakdown of tissues and organs as we age.
  • Immune Cell Function: Immune cells help protect the body from infections and diseases. They recognize harmful invaders and work to destroy them.

These cellular processes are critical for maintaining tissue and metabolic homeostasis and promoting healthy aging.
